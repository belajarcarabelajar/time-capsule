# ⏳ Time Capsule

> Send messages, memories, and moments to your future self or loved ones.

## About

Time Capsule is an open source project that allows you to capture and preserve memories, messages, or moments in time — to be unlocked at a future date by yourself or someone you love.

## Features

- 📝 Write messages to your future self
- 📅 Schedule delivery at a specific date
- 🔒 Keep memories private or share with others
- 🌍 Open source & community-driven
